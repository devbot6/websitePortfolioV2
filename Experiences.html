This was an after school lab focused primarily on Robotics that I made. I Made this because my school didn't have a robotics team that was open to everyone but I still wanted to do robotics.

Specifics

So I got together a group of people and asked the robotics teacher at KCAL if there were any spare pieces that we could use and he allowed us ot use some spare VEX parts to make a new robot which is what we worked in every tuesday and Thursday for the rest of the year.

U-Chassis

his is the type of chassis we decided to use for our design as it allowed for a great center of gravity and made it easy to line up cubes.

6 Bar Lift Design

We chose this design for the lift because we wanted the ability to pick things up can keep them in allignment with the ground. This desgin allows us to pick up cubes and ensure they are parallel to the ground as the lift.
