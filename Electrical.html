Smart Arduino Coke Car

I hand wired, put together, and programmed this car without instructions as well as implemented it into another project known as the coke car porject which can be seen on the CAD page in which I made some of the pieces in there. 

Process

So to begin I laid out all the parts and identifed the 2 main panels that everythings sits on. I then used my best judgement to screw the parts into where I believed they belonged. After I finsihed building the car and attaching the sensors I began turning it into a coke car which started with replacing the top panel for a CAD top panel this way I could drill holes into the CAD one without damaging the real one. This was so that I could attach the CAD cup holder I made once the CAD cupholder was attached all that was left was progamming which I essentially made it sense the color baclk and made it follow a black line to a designated location in which the coke would be delivered.
